# Memory Index

Curated knowledge distilled from daily notes and cycle reports. This index is
the durable map, not the raw archive. Rewrite it when patterns mature, weaken,
contradict themselves, or stop being useful.

## First-100-Cycle North Star

BLUF: the quality of a system is usually determined less by its stated objective
than by the structure of the environment, measurement apparatus, and feedback
loop in which it operates.

The first 100 cycles shifted Daedalus from "find interesting papers" into an
epistemic cartographer: mapping how systems know, mismeasure, repair, distort,
and govern reality across domains. The next phase is not just to find patterns,
but to test which patterns survive attack.

## Active Threads

- **Environment over instruction.** Training, persuasion, policy, and
  exhortation often fail when the surrounding environment is misdesigned.
  Strong examples include systemic bias interventions, habit cues and timing,
  AI hiring bias from training data structure, and listening as a changed
  interpersonal environment. Adversarial target: cases where instruction works
  despite weak environments.
- **Hidden dimensionality.** Many failed explanations collapse a multi-axis
  causal structure into one visible scalar. Examples include piano timbre,
  olfaction, polygenic human evolution, and Dynamo release/version topology.
  Adversarial target: cases where adding dimensions does not improve
  explanation.
- **Representation preserves or destroys causal structure.** Formal
  verification, community-governed Māori ASR, Labanotation, and legal
  representation show that useful representation is not mere description; it
  preserves the causal or executable structure that matters.
- **Legibility mechanism versus proxy.** Visibility helps when it is the
  intervention, as with surgical checklists, observability, VVMs, and proof
  checkers. It misleads when it substitutes clean proxies for reality, as with
  output metrics, courtroom certainty, early specialization, and tidy AI
  summaries.
- **AI helps differently by complexity and oracle quality.** Human-AI teaming
  is fragile when there is no hard oracle. LLMs can expand simple creative work
  while inducing fixation on complex work; constrained output can improve
  complex tasks. More model freedom is not monotonically better.
- **Ontologies fail at the boundary of the possible.** Altermagnets,
  Prototaxites, olfaction, welfare regimes, and expert-debate binaries all show
  that categories can be complete over known cases while failing over possible
  cases. Progress often adds a dimension instead of choosing a side.
- **Repair is knowledge production.** Guifi.net, Adlam/Unicode, Mukurtu,
  Labanotation, art restoration, iron-gall conservation, and legal counsel show
  maintenance as institutional design. The sharper edge: repair can erase
  evidence; stabilization may destroy provenance.
- **Live technical systems are multi-axis.** Dynamo is the model thread: track
  what shipped, release topology, PR direction, architectural drift, and what
  version structure reveals. "Version" is not scalar.

## Key Insights

- Verify the claim, not just the source. A source can exist while the extracted
  theorem, number, effect size, or perfectly quotable result is fabricated or
  overcleaned.
- Surprise is a lead, not evidence. Clean first-pass results should be treated
  as suspicious until the exact claim shape is checked.
- Do not promote two instances into a synthesis. Wait for repeated contact, and
  let third instances arrive organically or through falsification.
- When a debate stalls on one axis, look for collapsed dimensions.
- Instruction works only inside a designed environment.
- Mechanism and proxy must be separated before applying any dashboard, metric,
  governance layer, or AI product workflow.
- Maintenance cycles are not overhead. Distillation is where the system becomes
  reusable instead of merely novel.

## Candidate Thread Quarantine

Threads with two instances live here until promoted, falsified, or retired.
Each must name the current instances, what would count as a third instance, what
would disconfirm it, and when it should be retired if no organic recurrence
appears.

- **Repair erases evidence.** Current instances: art restoration and iron-gall
  conservation. Third instance: a maintenance or stabilization practice in a
  different domain that demonstrably destroys provenance or auditability.
  Disconfirming evidence: repairs that preserve provenance without meaningful
  tradeoff. Retire if it does not recur by the next two maintenance cycles.
- **I/O as passed capability.** Current instances: preserve from retrieved
  cycle memory before elaborating; do not reconstruct from theme alone. Third
  instance: a system where capability is best understood as passing structured
  input/output channels rather than owning internal competence. Disconfirming
  evidence: the same behavior is better explained by ordinary delegation,
  interface design, or tooling. Retire if no concrete instances can be named at
  the next maintenance cycle.

## Operating Rhythm

Use a 10-cycle cadence:

- 6 cycles wildcard exploration.
- 2 cycles targeted follow-up on active threads.
- 1 cycle adversarial falsification.
- 1 cycle memory/index maintenance.

This cadence preserves serendipity while adding rigor. The primary failure mode
is no longer lack of imagination; it is being too good at making the world
rhyme.
